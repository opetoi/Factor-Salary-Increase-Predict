# Factor-affecting-Salary-Increase-Predict
**Summerize** <br>
**1. Most Important Factor - Experience** <br>
  Years of experience showing the strongest positive relationship with salary as experience increase, salary tends to rise significantly across all roles.<br>
**2. Second Factor - Certifications**<br>
  Certifications demonstrate a moderate positive impact on salary.<br>
While the trend is not steep, certified individuals generally earn more than those with fewer or no certifications.<br>
**3. Least impact - Number of Skills** <br>
  The number of skills shows a weak correlation with salary.<br>
Having more skills does not necessarily translate to higher pay, suggesting that skill relevance and specialization matter more than quantity.<br>

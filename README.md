**1. Most Important Factor - Experience
**Years of experience showing the strongest positive relationship with salary as experience increase, salary tends to rise significantly across all roles.
**2. Second Factor - Certifications
**Certifications demonstrate a moderate positive impact on salary.
While the trend is not steep, certified individuals generally earn more than those with fewer or no certifications.
**3. Least impact - Number of Skills
**The number of skills shows a weak correlation with salary.
Having more skills does not necessarily translate to higher pay, suggesting that skill relevance and specialization matter more than quantity.
